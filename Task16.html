URI Encoding Demonstration

Original URL (Incorrect):

https://example.com/search?query=HTML & CSS

Encoded URL (Correct):

https://example.com/search?query=HTML%20%26%20CSS

Explanation:

In URLs, special characters need to be encoded to ensure proper transmission:
- Space is encoded as %20
- Ampersand (&) is encoded as %26
- Other special characters also have their percent-encoded equivalents

More Examples:

  • Space: " " → %20
  • Plus: "+" → %2B
  • Hash: "#" → %23
  • Question mark: "?" → %3F
  • Equals: "=" → %3D
